Details & care

Sharp today. Sharper in a year.

A great knife is an investment. RSC is designed to stay with you in the kitchen— and stay sharp—with just simple care.

  • Hone, don’t fight. A few passes on a honing steel before service keeps the edge aligned and ready.
  • Hand-wash only. Clean quickly, dry completely, and store safely. Your blade will thank you.
  • Use the cover. Slip on the Harm Protection cover when storing or setting aside to prevent accidents.
